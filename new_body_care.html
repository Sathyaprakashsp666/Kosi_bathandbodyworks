About New Body Care from Bath & Body Works.

Welcome to the world of new body care, where you can find the latest releases, seasonal fragrances and freshest products.

What’s new? First off: THE FRAGRANCES. We’re always experimenting with new scents and blends, and once the weather changes: seasonal fragrances. Yes! As soon as spring comes around, we’ll fill our shelves (and webpages) with new florals, beachy scents and refreshing vibes. And of course, we’ll have new fragrances for summer, fall and winter (totally excited for holiday scents!).

Now that you’ve checked out the newest scents (and hopefully found something you love), it’s time to update your body care routine with the latest goodies.

  • Cleanse: We love a new shower gel or moisturizing body wash to wash away stress and refresh our senses.
  • Moisturize: Next, lock in hydration and get super-soft skin with a body lotion or body cream.
  • Mist: Finish off your routine with a light-as-air fine fragrance mist – a matching scent is always great. Want something for a special occasion? Try a perfume, only available in our most luxurious scents.
  • Repeat: And that’s it! Three simple and easy steps to smelling great all day.

Looking to indulge a little more? Explore body scrubs – not only are they the definition of pampering, but they’re great for exfoliating your skin. And while you’re getting some R&R, soak and chill with our super-luxe and super fluffy bubble bath. (Don’t forget a glass of wine and a great podcast.)

Now that you’ve found all things new, what about scents you haven’t seen for a while? Head over to retired fragrances and reunite with some old faves!
